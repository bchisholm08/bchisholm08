Welcome to my personal website! I am a researcher who received their undergraduate degree from the University of Minnesota, earning my B.S. in Psychology in August 2025. I am currently working as a Research Professional in Dr. Andrew Oxenham's Auditory Perception and Cognition Laboratory.

My research interests involve cognitive perception, along with statistics and computational neuroscience. I am particularly interested in how we can use behavioral data and statistical methods to explore and explain human behavior. Explore my work and feel free to get in touch!
