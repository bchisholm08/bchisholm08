My Research

Research Interests

I am a data-driven researcher interested in how we can leverage cognitive science and data science to help understand how does our brain interface with and understand the world? To investigate this, I've been training in gathering and analysis of multi-modal data across various labs, including EEG, pupillometry, and behavioral data from human participants.

My technical expertise is centered around R and MATLAB, where I’ve developed end-to-end pipelines for signal processing and statistical modeling. I am passionate about designing robust experiments and visualizing complex data to create clear, compelling stories about human cognition.
