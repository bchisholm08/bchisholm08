My Research

Research Focus

I am a data-driven researcher interested in the intersection of cognitive science and data science, and motivated by asking, how does our brain interface with the world? To investigate this, I have been involved in multiple labs using a multi-modal approach, collecting and analyzing EEG, pupillometry, and behavioral data from human participants. My technical experience is centered around R and MATLAB, where I’ve developed end-to-end pipelines for signal processing and statistical modeling. I'm passionate about designing robust experiments and visualizing complex data in ways that reveal clear, compelling stories about human cognition.
