APC Lab @ UMN-TC

Welcome to my personal website! I am an undergraduate student at the University of Minnesota, majoring with a B.S. in Psychology. My research interests involve any work that explains how we function and percieve the world around us. I believe we are at an optimal time in science to take advantage of technology we have available to unlock the secrets of the brain. For me, answering these questions involves cognitive psychology, biology, statistics, and computational neuroscience. These are all areas I am working on developing as I wrap up my undergraduate experience and look towards graduate school.

Currently, I am working as a research assistant in Dr. Andrew Oxenham's Auditory Perception and Cognition Lab and Dr. Jean-Paul Noel's Neuroscience Laboratory.



Explore my website to learn about my academic background, projects I've worked on, and my goals for future graduate studies.
