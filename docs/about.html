About Me

Hello, my name is Brady!

Recent graduate from the University of Minnesota Twin-Cities with a B.S. in Psychology.

Working as a researcher in the Auditory Perception and Cognition lab at U of M Twin-Cities!

Research Interests

I am a data-driven researcher interested in the intersection of cognitive science and data science, and motivated asking: How does our brain interface with the world? To investigate this, I have been involved in multiple labs using a multi-modal approach, collecting and analyzing EEG, pupillometry, and behavioral data from human participants. My software experience is centered around R and MATLAB, where I’ve developed end-to-end analytical pipelines for signal processing and statistical modeling. I'm passionate about designing robust experiments and visualizing complex data in ways that reveal clear, compelling stories about human cognition.
